# 🚀 Launch Guide — Tomorrow Morning
*Step by step. ~2 hours total.*

---

## STEP 1: Build Your Packs (5 minutes)
Open PowerShell and run:
```powershell
cd C:\Users\efenn\.openclaw\workspace\etsy-shop
.\pack-builder.ps1
```
This will:
- Copy all design files into clean pack folders
- Add a README/license to each pack
- Create ZIP files in `ready-to-upload\`

Check the output — it'll tell you if any files are missing.

---

## STEP 2: Create Your Etsy Seller Account (15 minutes)
1. Go to: **https://www.etsy.com/sell**
2. Click "Get started"
3. Choose shop language/currency/country
4. **Shop name ideas:**
   - `SwagNuggetsDesigns` ✅ (recommended)
   - `NuggetPrintCo`
   - `SwagPrintStudio`
5. Set up billing (credit card required for listing fees)
6. Connect PayPal or bank account for payouts

---

## STEP 3: Set Up Shop Branding (10 minutes)
Use files from `D:\Pictures\OG Content\`:
- **Shop Icon (500x500px):** Use `Swag_Nuggets_Logo_final.png` or `FaviconLarge.png`
- **Shop Banner (3360x840px):** Use `logofull.png` or create a simple one in Canva
- **Shop bio:** Copy from `BUSINESS_PLAN.md`

---

## STEP 4: Create Listings (60 minutes, ~7-8 min each)
For each pack:
1. Click "Add a listing"
2. **Photos:** Use existing product mockup PNGs from `D:\Pictures\T-shirt designs\Pictures\` (t-shirt mockups)
   - Add 3-5 photos: design preview, mockup on shirt, example colors
3. **Title:** Copy from `listings\listing-templates.md`
4. **Category:** Art & Collectibles → Digital Prints
   OR Craft Supplies → Patterns & How To → Digital Patterns
5. **Price:** As listed in templates
6. **Quantity:** Digital = Unlimited (check "This is a digital file")
7. **Upload file:** Upload the ZIP from `ready-to-upload\`
8. **Description:** Copy from templates
9. **Tags:** Copy 13 tags from templates
10. **Shipping:** N/A for digital
11. Publish!

**Priority order (do these first):**
1. Pack 1 (Funny Humor — broadest appeal)
2. Pack 4 (Christmas — seasonal premium)
3. Pack 6 (Stickers — Cricut crowd is huge on Etsy)
4. Pack 2 (Christian — very loyal niche)
5. Rest in any order

---

- Consider: r/EtsySellers, r/Etsy on Reddit for feedback
