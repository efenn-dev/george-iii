# Fiverr Quick Response Templates

Professional, warm templates for common buyer interactions. Keep responses under 200 characters where possible.

---

## 1. New Inquiry - General Greeting

```
Hi there! Thanks for reaching out. I'd love to help with your project. Could you share a few details about what you're looking for?
```
*Character count: 141*

---

## 2. Asking for Project Details

```
Thanks for your interest! To give you an accurate quote, could you share: scope, timeline, and any specific requirements? Looking forward to working together!
```
*Character count: 176*

---

## 3. Custom Quote Response

```
Thanks for the details! Based on your requirements, I can deliver this for $[PRICE] within [TIMELINE]. Ready to start whenever you are. Questions? Just ask!
```
*Character count: 155*

## 4. Order Received Confirmation

```
Order received! 🎉 I'm on it and will deliver by [DATE]. I'll keep you updated on progress. Thanks for choosing me!
```

## 8. Review Request (Polite, After Delivery)

```
Hope you're happy with the delivery! If you have a moment, a quick review would mean the world. It helps me grow and helps other buyers find quality work. Thanks so much! 🙏
```
*Character count: 196*

---

## Quick Tips

- Personalize each response with the buyer's name when possible
- Adjust tone slightly based on the project's formality
- Always respond within 24 hours (faster = better ranking)
- Use emojis sparingly—they add warmth but don't overdo it
- Keep it professional but approachable
