## 5 Key Findings

### 1. Critical Site Speed Issues (Impact: HIGH)
Page load times average 4.8 seconds on mobile, significantly above Google's recommended 2.5-second threshold. Image optimization and render-blocking JavaScript are primary culprits, directly impacting mobile rankings and user experience.

### 2. Thin Product Descriptions (Impact: HIGH)
47% of product pages contain fewer than 150 words of unique content. Manufacturer descriptions are duplicated across multiple SKUs, triggering Google's "helpful content" penalties and limiting organic visibility for long-tail keywords.

### 3. Poor URL Structure & Navigation (Impact: MEDIUM)
E-commerce platform generates dynamic URLs with excessive parameters (e.g., `/product?id=2847&ref=cat`). No breadcrumb schema markup present. Site architecture lacks clear category hierarchies, impeding crawl efficiency.

### 4. Missing Local SEO Optimization (Impact: MEDIUM)
Despite operating two fulfillment centers with local delivery options, Google Business Profiles are unclaimed. No location-specific landing pages exist, forfeiting "pet supplies near me" search traffic valued at an estimated 12,000 monthly searches.

### 5. Inadequate Internal Linking Strategy (Impact: MEDIUM)
Product pages average 1.2 internal links each. Related products, complementary categories, and buying guides are rarely cross-referenced, reducing page authority distribution and limiting session duration metrics.
