## Content Strategy Notes

### Monday — Motivation Focus
- **Goal:** Drive morning foot traffic
- **Tone:** Energizing, community-oriented
- **CTA:** Engagement (comment your order)
- **Cross-promote:** Story links to menu page

### Tuesday — Visual Satisfaction
- **Goal:** Showcase craft/quality, reach new audiences via algorithm
- **Tone:** Aesthetic, calming, skill-focused
- **CTA:** Soft (mention "the leaf" when ordering)
- **Cross-promote:** Pin to Pinterest, YouTube Shorts

### Wednesday — Human Connection
- **Goal:** Build brand trust, highlight ethical practices
- **Tone:** Personal, story-driven, values-based
- **CTA:** Engagement (suggest next feature)
- **Cross-promote:** LinkedIn post about ethical sourcing
