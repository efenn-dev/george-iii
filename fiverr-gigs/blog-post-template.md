## SEO Checklist

**Before Publishing:**
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Primary keyword in at least one H2
- [ ] Meta description includes primary keyword (155 chars max)
- [ ] URL slug is short and includes primary keyword
- [ ] Alt text on all images includes keywords where natural
- [ ] At least 2-3 internal links to relevant pages
- [ ] 1-2 external links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Reading level appropriate for audience
- [ ] Scannable with headers, bullets, and short paragraphs
- [ ] Conclusion includes clear CTA
