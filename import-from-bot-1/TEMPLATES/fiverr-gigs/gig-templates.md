## Gig Writing Tips

### Title Formula
```
I Will [Benefit] + [Differentiator] + [Timeframe/Outcome]
```

**Good:** "I Will Write SEO-Optimized Blog Posts That Engage and Convert"
**Bad:** "Blog Post Writing Service - Fast Delivery"

### Description Structure
1. **Hook** — Problem statement or bold promise (1-2 sentences)
2. **What you get** — Bullet list of deliverables
3. **Why choose you** — Differentiators with emojis
4. **Call to action** — Clear next step

### Pricing Psychology
- Basic: Entry point, shows you're affordable
- Standard: Most popular (highlight this)
- Premium: Anchor price, makes Standard look reasonable
